About This Project

Innovating the Future of Electric Mobility

Project Overview

This project focuses on advancing electric motorcycles by modifying and upgrading key components such as the battery, motor, display, and charging system. The goal is to create a smaller, more reliable, efficient, and powerful electric motorcycle that is suitable for modern transportation needs. It contributes to clean energy usage, sustainability, and smart mobility solutions in urban and rural areas alike.
